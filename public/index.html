Morocco Travel Tips

Make the most of your journey through Morocco

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March to May) and Fall (September to November) offer the most pleasant temperatures for exploring Morocco.

Language

Arabic and Berber are official languages, but French is widely spoken in cities. Learn basic greetings in Arabic.

Currency

The Moroccan Dirham (MAD) is the local currency. ATMs are widely available in cities but bring cash for rural areas.

Food & Drink

Try tagine, couscous, and mint tea. Street food is delicious but stick to busy stalls with high turnover.
